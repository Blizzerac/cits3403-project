How it all works

Posting ReQuests

Post ReQuests, calling for aid from your fellow adventurers in many different contexts. Whether it's seeking guidance on a challenging foe, requesting assistance in gathering resources, or rallying support for a noble cause, our application provides a platform for users to connect, collaborate, and embark on shared adventures together.

Searching for ReQuests

Search for ReQuests to answer, delving into a diverse array of tasks spanning various realms and challenges. From uncovering hidden treasures to unraveling ancient mysteries, the search feature serves as a gateway to endless opportunities, guiding adventurers towards their next epic undertaking. With intuitive search filters and dynamic exploration tools, users can embark on a ReQuest-finding journey tailored to their interests and aspirations.

Answering ReQuests:The FUN part

Answer ReQuests, earn gold, and rise to the top of the monthly leaderboard. Endeavour to be the best to earn a monthly prize! Proof must be submitted when responding, to ensure the legitimacy and fairness of the competition. As you embark on your ReQuesting journey, not only do you have the chance to earn rewards and recognition, but you also contribute to a vibrant community of adventurers. Share your expertise, forge new connections, and make lasting memories along the way.
